ABOUT US

ECELL GECG HISTORY

The Entrepreneurship Cell, at GEC, Gandhinagar was founded in 2022 by Param Radadiya with the motto of ‘Explore, Experience, Experiment’. Currently, we are a team of 25, 2 overall Coordinators, and many enthusiastic students studying at GEC Gandhinagar, sharing a common vision. The team meet ups happen in ‘SARJAN- The pre-incubator’, where the strategy to uplift the flame of startups across the institute originates.
