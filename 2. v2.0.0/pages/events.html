Events we Organise

ECELL GECG organises various events throughout the year.

Ideathon

Ideathon is one of the most successful flagship event of ECELL GECG, organised every year to give the platform for the enthusiasts which carries innovative ideas which they can convert into a startup.
Till date ECELL GECG has organised 3 succesful ideathon & preideathon events.

Upbeat Upstart

Upbeat Upstart is the pre-ideathon event started by ECELL GECG to promote entrepreneurship culture among the students to take the institute a step forward.
An informative session like mind mapping was conducted to give a visual representation of an idea.
